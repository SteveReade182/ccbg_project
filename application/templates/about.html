Welcome! CCBG race using the iRacing simulator that hosts such drivers like "World Champion" & Red Bull F1 team Max Verstappen, Mclaren driver Lando Norris and Nascar driver Dale Earnhardt Jr to name a few. It is a very realistic simulator, laser scanning tracks and modelling car physics to make the ultimate racing experience available to all from the comforts of your own home! We have been together since 2019 and are always excited to have new drivers join us. We mainly compete in endurance racing but are open to suggestions from members. If you have any questions feel free to contact us, otherwise enjoy the site!!
