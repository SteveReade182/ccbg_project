Welcome! CCBG race using the iRacing simulator that hosts such drivers like "World Champion" & Red Bull F1 team Max Verstappen, Mclaren driver Lando Norris and Nascar driver Dale Earnhardt Jr to name a few. It is a very realistic simulator, laser scanning tracks and modelling car physics to make the ultimate racing experience available to all from the comforts of your own home! We have been together since 2019 and are always excited to have new drivers join us. We mainly compete in endurance racing but are open to suggestions from members. If you have any questions feel free to contact us, otherwise enjoy the site!!

Fanatec - Wheels that you'll love more than racing!

One of the most popular brands used by many sim racers is "Fanatec" and the price isn't too crazy! They offer entry to mid-level hardware that has the likes of force feedback (to feel the bumps in the road), loadcell pedals (to make the brake feel realistic) and mny choices of steering wheels, some of which are made out of carbon fibre and the new bmw m4 wheel is a REAL car steering wheel!

The Backbone of a sim rig!

Sim Lab are one of many companies that offer aluminuim profile rigs! The reason you want one of these types of sim rig is that it is rigid! No flex means the steering and pedal inputs and feedback are more accurate so you feel more! Also bolting on a bucket seat and your monitor of choice with the many other optional extras and you have an awesome looking and functional simulator!

iRacing - the world's most competitive sim!

What's iRacing.com? iRacing is the ultimate place to go racing online. From NASCAR, to IndyCar, to sports cars and Grand Prix racing, iRacing has it all. All you need is a PC, a gaming wheel or game pad and an Internet connection. iRacing organizes all of the racing for you, or you can choose to host your own race.
